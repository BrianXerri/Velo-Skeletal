Easy to install

Step 1

Check which side your roller chain is on, if you need to swap the position, remove the side plate and slot it back into the groove on the correct side as shown

Step 2

Carefully slide your assembled Velo onto your roller blind bracket taking care not damage the magnets

Enjoy!
